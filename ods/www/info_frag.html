As of mid October, 2025, I am working on two paid software projects. This shows how many hours and days I am ahead or behind. With both projects, I am paid a fixed fee per month. In one case, there is an agreed-upon $ / hour, and thus an agreed upon hours per month. In the other, I try to keep it the same $ / hour as the first one, even though there is not an agreed-upon amount of work.

"As of" in the table is the time of my hours-tracking spreadsheets. "As of" below the table is "now," when you loaded the page. The spreadsheets are .ODS or LibreOffice Calc. Then I convert to CSV and POST to kwynn.com.
